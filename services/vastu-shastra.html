What a Vastu Analysis Covers

Vastu Shastra works with the five elements (Panch Tatva) — Earth, Water, Fire, Air, and Space — and their relationship with the eight cardinal directions. When these elements are balanced in your living or working space, things tend to flow more smoothly. When they're not, the effects can show up as financial blocks, health issues, or interpersonal friction.

  • Directional Analysis

    Every direction in your home or office governs specific aspects of life. North affects finances, East affects health and growth, South-West affects relationships and stability. We check whether the key rooms, entrances, and elements in your space align with these directional principles.

  • Room-by-Room Assessment

    The position of your bedroom, kitchen, bathroom, puja room, main entrance, and workspace all matter. We assess each room's placement relative to direction and recommend adjustments where needed.

  • Element Balance

    Water (fountains, tanks, bathrooms), fire (kitchen, electrical panels), air (ventilation, windows), and earth (heavy storage, level of ground) all need to be in their correct zones. Imbalances here are among the most common Vastu issues — and often the easiest to fix.

  • External Factors

    T-junctions facing your home, neighbouring structures, cut plots, and road positions also affect Vastu energy. We assess these external factors as part of the consultation.

This is important: most Vastu corrections do not require breaking walls, demolishing rooms, or rebuilding your home. Changes in room usage, furniture placement, colour adjustments, element additions (like water features or plants), and specific remedial items can address the majority of issues.
