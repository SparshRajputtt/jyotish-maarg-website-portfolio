The Story Behind Jyotish Maarg

14 years. 5,000+ consultations. One approach — honest guidance.

About Shashank Shekhar Sharma

I didn't grow up planning to become an astrologer.

Like most people, I had questions about my own life — why certain things were happening, why some periods felt effortless while others felt like pushing against a wall. I started reading about Vedic astrology as a way to understand those patterns, and once I began studying birth charts seriously, I realised this wasn't just theory. It was remarkably specific.

That was in 2012. I was living in Haridwar — a city where Vedic knowledge isn't just studied, it's lived. I found teachers who taught me the classical systems: Parashari, Jaimini, and the Nadi methods. I studied full-time. I practised by reading charts for family, then friends, then friends of friends. Word spread because the readings were accurate and the advice was practical.

Over the next several years, what started as a personal inquiry became a full-time practice. I named it Jyotish Maarg — literally, "the path of light." Not because astrology gives you all the answers, but because it gives you enough light to see the road ahead more clearly.

Today, I've completed over 5,000 consultations for people across India and in several countries. My clients include working professionals, business owners, families looking for marriage guidance, and homeowners dealing with Vastu concerns. The questions are always different, but my approach stays the same:

Study the chart carefully. Explain what I see honestly. Give practical recommendations. Never use fear.

I still operate from Haridwar, though most of my consultations now happen over video and phone calls. Whether you're sitting across from me or calling from another city, the depth of the reading is the same.

If you're considering a consultation, I'd suggest starting with a specific question rather than a general reading. The more focused your question, the more useful the session will be.

Core Beliefs

How I Approach Astrology

✦

There are a few things I believe strongly, and they shape every consultation I do:

1. Your chart is not your fate.

A birth chart shows tendencies, strengths, and challenges — not a fixed script. You always have choices. My job is to show you which choices are more likely to work in your favour, and when.

2. Fear has no place in a consultation.

If I see a difficult period in your chart, I'll tell you about it — but I'll also tell you how long it lasts, what it actually means, and what you can do about it. I will never manufacture fear to sell remedies.

3. Clarity matters more than complexity.

I could use technical jargon to sound impressive, but that doesn't help you. My goal is for you to leave every consultation with a clear understanding of what's happening in your chart and what to do next.

4. Remedies should be practical.

I recommend remedies that are simple, affordable, and rooted in the Vedic tradition. I don't sell gemstones. I don't push expensive rituals. If a mantra or a simple change in routine can help, that's what I'll suggest.

5. Every chart deserves full attention.

I don't use automated software reports. Before every consultation, I personally study your chart. This takes time, but it's the only way to give you genuinely useful guidance.
